02. Chennai Book Fair 2023

📜 46th Edition Overview

Inaugurated by Hon'ble Chief Minister MK Stalin, the 2023 Book Fair was a milestone cultural event spanning 17 days at the YMCA College Grounds.

🏛️ Historical Milestones

2009

32nd Fair at St. George's. Reached 1M visitors and ₹270M in sales for the first time.

2013

36th Fair moved to YMCA. Featured 747 stalls and 1 million+ titles.

2016

Flood Recovery: Rescheduled to June. Despite ₹25Cr industry loss, it drew major crowds.

2023

46th Fair: International segment added with publishers from 40+ countries.

✨ Special 2023 Features

  • First-ever International Book Fair segment
  • Dedicated Queer Publishing House stall
  • 100th Kalaignar Award Presentation
  • 200+ Completely New Stalls
  • Author Book Readings & Kids Section
